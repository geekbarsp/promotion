THE DEV BEYOND THE SCREEN

Video game development is more than just "making games." It is an interdisciplinary field that pushes the boundaries of hardware, artificial intelligence, and human psychology to create digital worlds that feel alive.

CAREER PATHWAYS

👑

AAA STUDIO

Work for giants like Rockstar or Ubisoft. Specialize in one specific area (like water physics or character hair) on massive, multi-million dollar projects.

🐺

INDIE DEV

The "Lone Wolf" route. Wear every hat—coder, artist, and marketer. High risk, but total creative freedom and 100% ownership.

🏢

ADVERGAMING

Creating interactive experiences for brands. High demand in marketing agencies for mobile apps and web-based games.

🔬

SERIOUS GAMES

Games for good. Developing simulations for medical training, military strategy, or complex educational tools.
